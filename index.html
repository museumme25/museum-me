About the App

Museum.me is a mobile application designed to help users discover museums, galleries, and cultural sites around the world. Whether you're planning a trip or exploring your local area, Museum.me provides comprehensive information about cultural destinations to enhance your experience.

Features

  • Browse hundreds of museums and cultural venues across 25+ cities worldwide
  • Search and filter venues by location and category
  • View detailed information including opening hours, admission prices, and descriptions
  • User-friendly interface designed for easy discovery
  • Regular updates with new venues and destinations

Third-Party Advertising

Museum.me uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized advertising. This includes:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier for advertising purposes)

For more information about how Google uses data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
